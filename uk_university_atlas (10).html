UK Application Process

1
Initial Counselling Session
First meeting to understand the student's goals, budget, and eligibility, and shortlist suitable universities and courses.
2
Required Documents to Process Application
Transcript, Provisional, Marksheet, Degree certificate, Character certificate, English proficiency test certificate (IELTS / PTE / Duolingo), CV, LOR, MOI, and Work experience certificate (if any).
3
Application to Departure
Documents verification→ Process application→ Receive offer letter→ Apply NOC→ TB test certificate→ Pre-CAS interview→ Pay tuition fees→ Show maintenance funds→ CAS letter→ Visa checklist & immigration history forms→ IHS & visa fee payment→ Biometric appointment→ UKVI credibility interview→ Visa decision→ Apply e-visa & share code→ Pre-departure sessions→ Departure planning
